    Essay Requirement

    For the KAM4 Scholarship, we require three essays of at least 1000 words a piece. Below you will find four essay topics, of which you must choose three to submit. When you have choosen which three you are going to write, type your essay in the corresponding box. Please leave one blank line between your paragraghs. You do not have to worry about indenting. Hint: it may be easier to type your essay in Google Docs or a Word document and then copy and paste your essay into the submission box. Google Docs and Word do a great job at keeping up with your word count for you!:

  • What does giving mean to you and why? In the previous year, how have you given back? To whom did you give back and why?
  • How will the KAM4 Scholarship help you in your educational endeavors?
  • Explain the process and theory of sowing and reaping. What does sowing and reaping mean to you? How have you seen the principle of sowing and reaping in your life?
  • Why do you desire a higher education? What are your goals, inspirations, challenges, motivations and motivators? Provide a brief description of any special circumstances you feel should be considered by the Board of Directors.
