    Essay Requirement

    For the KAM4 Scholarship, we require one essay that is between 500 and 1200 words. Below you will find the following information you should write about in your essay. When you are writing/inputting your essay, please leave one blank line between your paragraghs. You do not have to worry about indenting. Hint: it may be easier to type your essay in Google Docs or a Word document and then copy and paste your essay into the submission box. Google Docs and Word do a great job at keeping up with your word count for you!

  • Who are you?
  • Where are you from?
  • What college are you planning to attend?
  • Your reason for selecting the college that you chose to attend?
  • What major/degree study courses do you want to obtain?
  • Your reason for selecting the major/degree that you chose to study in?
  • What are your goals, inspirations, challenges, and motivations?
  • How do you plan to use your degree in the future to help others?
  • What does "giving" mean to you?
  • How have you given back, to whom, where and why?
  • How will this scholarship help you in your educational endeavors?
